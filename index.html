Our services

At Hacks for parents, we are committed to supporting new parents on their exciting parenting journey. Our team of child psychology and parenting experts offer resources and guidance to help you navigate the challenges and celebrate the accomplishments of parenthood.

Be a wonderful Parent

You will be a great father

Your baby is going to be a change in your life and many fears arise. It is perfectly normal. "Will I make mistakes?" Will I be able to do it right?" The answer is yes. You will be capable, you will learn and your baby will be perfectly under your care. You just need patience and organization.

Get informed, without obsessions

Try to inform yourself as much as possible without obsessing. There are millions of books, advice for new parents, prenatal classes... Do not fall into the trap of information overload.

Listen to other parents… just and necessary

Experience is a degree, without a doubt. Other parents may recommend tips or ways to make homework easier. The risk is that they end up overwhelming you. Don't worry, new father, you will end up having your own method and you will be the one to advise other parents.

Am I holding you right?

Holding your baby in your arms is a wonderful feeling but at first it can be a little scary. As a new parent you will wonder if you are holding him correctly. There are several ways to do it:

  • Leaning on your forearm face down.
  • Leaning on your forearm face up.
  • On your chest, vertically and holding his head.
  • Avoid moving him too much even if it is playing to make him laugh.
